THE VISION

TRAVIALIST IS YOUR NEW ADVENTURE

The concept is simple. Traveling is one of the most popular activities around the world. At Travialist we create a new digital experience for you and your trip. Travel guides and books are outdated once you purchase them and allow only low interaction. It all began while a weekend trip to London. Searching for a bus stop from a tour operator, the map did not do its job. Missing streets and wrong data made hard to find the right location. Building on the idea of creating a unique, user orientated global travel guide, Travialist was born. We use state of the art technology to provide you and your friends a unique experience. Choose your lifestyle and connect with others. Share your activity and plan your new experience abroad. We build all the tools you need and are continuously improving our applications, web based and on mobile platforms. THE LIFESTYLE

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Travialist has lifetime values. We believe in a future travel market, where sustainability and growth develop together. Travialist will not be a source for the cheapest seat in an airplane or the low budget all-inclusive holiday. We love to show you offers relevant to your lifestyle and indicate transparent pricing. We do not intent to be an online travel agency but for sure, we can offer you remarkable experiences in partnership with market leaders!

As we at Travialist are always aware of what happens to our privacy and data in social networks and the internet, we intend to establish a white-hat practice. User Data is on of the most valuable goods in the digital age. It is necessary for many applications and the learning process of almost any search engine. Our Apps will track what you do, like and look out for. But we do it differently, because we can. All user-related data will be processed anonymous in order to guarantee you privacy and to comply with EU Data Protection Laws. By signing up for an account we will declare what exactly happens to you and your data and why we need it to do the way we plan. What we provide you is a unique user experience for every single stage of traveling by letting you own and control your data and privacy.

GLOBAL

Travialist is not just another directory of what could be good in a certain region. We intent to be the global source for traveling. We aim high, covering the whole world within the next few years is our primary goal.Therefor we count on user generated content, completed with a variety of articles relevant to different lifestyles from our author team. At the beginning, we cover 500+ destination, partnering with leading content and data provider.

CROWD INTELLIGENCE

We listen to the crowd!Our Policy and Terms of Use declare exactly what kind of interaction we are looking for. You are free to flag and thereby notify us if you encounter any suspicious activity. Contribute to Travialist and help us grow by writing your thoughts and the places you would see, or you already visited. It is a pleasure helping others to find the exact spot that matches. We implement our own algorithms and deliver your preferences using artificial intelligence, that never stops learning. Do not waste your time for research and use your experience on our platform.

CONTENT INTEGRITY

We are independent!Articles on our Platform are not scraped or copied from other pages. We have licensed travel content completed with author-written articles. We pay attribution to whom created the content on our platform as respect digital data ownership. All authors are real people and ready for interaction with you. The selection of our own content is based on interest and user request. We never receive any form of compensation for focusing on a certain city, listing or product; because we love to be different.
